Welcome to my blog! I've been meaning to start writing for a while, and I finally decided to just do it.

## Why I'm writing

Writing helps me think. When I sit down to explain something, I realize how much I don't actually understand — and that's valuable. This blog is as much for me as it is for anyone who stumbles across it.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Web development** — Next.js, TypeScript, React, and the ecosystem around them
- **Building things** — lessons learned from side projects and real work
- **Tools & workflows** — what I use, what I've tried, what I've abandoned
- **Thoughts** — occasionally just thinking out loud

## How this site is built

This blog is built with [Next.js 16](https://nextjs.org) and deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com). Posts are written in MDX — Markdown with JSX — which means I can embed interactive components right inside articles.

The source is on [GitHub](https://github.com/theanjali01/my-nextjs-app) and the domain is `anjalishrestha.com.np`, managed through Cloudflare.
