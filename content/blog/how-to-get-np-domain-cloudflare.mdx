Nepal offers free `.com.np` domains to Nepali citizens and organizations. This guide walks you through the entire process — from registration to having your site live at `yourname.com.np`.

## What you'll need

- A Nepali citizenship card or passport (for verification)
- A working email address
- About 30 minutes

---

## Step 1: Register at Mercantile (register.com.np)

The `.np` domain registry is managed by **Mercantile Communications**, Nepal's official ccTLD authority.

1. Go to **[register.com.np](https://register.com.np)**
2. Click **"Check Availability"** and search for your desired domain
3. If available, click **"Register"**
4. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Registrant Name** — your full legal name
   - **Organization** — optional (leave blank for personal domains)
   - **Email** — use a reliable address you check regularly
   - **Phone** — Nepali phone number

### Document requirements

You'll need to upload **one of**:

| Document | Accepted format |
|---|---|
| Citizenship card | Scanned front & back, PDF or JPG |
| Passport | Photo page, PDF or JPG |
| Company registration | For `.org.np` or `.net.np` |

5. Upload your document and submit
6. **Wait for approval** — this typically takes **1–5 business days** (manual review)
7. You'll receive a confirmation email with your domain login credentials

> **Tip:** Use a clear, well-lit scan. Blurry documents are the most common reason for rejection and delay.

## Step 2: Log in and set Cloudflare nameservers

Once approved, log in at `register.com.np` and update your nameservers to point to Cloudflare. This lets Cloudflare manage your DNS instead of Mercantile's basic panel.

### First, create a Cloudflare account

1. Go to **[cloudflare.com](https://cloudflare.com)** → **Sign Up** (free)
2. Click **"Add a Site"** → enter your domain (e.g. `anjalishrestha.com.np`)
3. Select the **Free plan**
4. Cloudflare will scan for existing DNS records — click **Continue**
5. Cloudflare gives you **two nameservers**, something like:
   ```
   abel.ns.cloudflare.com
   rose.ns.cloudflare.com
   ```
   Copy these — you'll need them in the next step.

### Update nameservers at register.com.np

1. Log back into your account at `register.com.np`
2. Go to **"My Domains"** → click your domain → **"Manage"**
3. Find the **Nameservers** section
4. Replace the existing nameservers with the two Cloudflare ones:
   ```
   abel.ns.cloudflare.com
   rose.ns.cloudflare.com
   ```
5. Save changes

> Nameserver propagation takes **up to 24 hours**, but usually completes within 1–2 hours for `.np` domains.

---

## Step 3: Add DNS records in Cloudflare

Now that Cloudflare controls your DNS, you can add records to point your domain wherever you want.

### For Vercel (recommended)

Go to **Cloudflare Dashboard → your domain → DNS → Add record**

**Root domain (`anjalishrestha.com.np`):**
```
Type:    A
Name:    @
Value:   76.76.21.21
Proxy:   OFF (grey cloud) ← important!
TTL:     Auto
```

**www subdomain:**
```
Type:    CNAME
Name:    www
Value:   cname.vercel-dns.com
Proxy:   OFF (grey cloud)
TTL:     Auto
```

> Keep the **orange proxy cloud OFF**. Vercel manages its own SSL via Let's Encrypt. If Cloudflare proxies traffic too, you'll get SSL certificate conflicts.

---

## Step 4: Add the domain in Vercel

1. Go to your **Vercel project → Settings → Domains**
2. Type in `anjalishrestha.com.np` → **Add**
3. Also add `www.anjalishrestha.com.np`
4. If Vercel says **"Verification Needed"** (because your domain was previously on another Vercel account), add a TXT record:
   ```
   Type:    TXT
   Name:    _vercel
   Value:   vc-domain-verify=... (copy from Vercel)
   Proxy:   OFF
   ```
5. Click **Refresh** in Vercel — it should turn green within a few minutes

---

## Step 5: Set up automatic www → root redirect

In Vercel, set `anjalishrestha.com.np` as the primary domain. Vercel will automatically redirect `www` to the root (or vice versa — your choice).

In **Vercel → Domains**, drag or set `www.anjalishrestha.com.np` as the redirect target. Done!

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| "Verification Needed" in Vercel | Add the `_vercel` TXT record at Cloudflare |
| Domain not resolving | Wait up to 24h for nameserver propagation; check with `dig NS anjalishrestha.com.np` |
| SSL error | Make sure Cloudflare proxy is **OFF** for Vercel domains |
| Application rejected | Re-upload clearer document scans to register.com.np |
| Nameserver change not saving | Contact Mercantile support at `hostmaster@mos.com.np` |
